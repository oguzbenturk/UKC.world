04  Why academies stay

Not a platform.
A practice.

  1. I.

    It speaks your language.

    Kite, wing, foil, e-foil, SUP. L1 to L5. Supervision sessions. Downwinders, packages, memberships. Plannivo was built from the inside of a Duotone pro center — not adapted from a yoga-studio tool.

  2. II.

    It respects your instructors.

    Instructors see their schedule, their students, their rate, their commission — but never the lesson gross amount. Payroll is automatic. Trust stays intact.

  3. III.

    It follows the season.

    Closed in winter? Switch the shop into direct-link mode and only handle inbound. Peak July? Multi-instructor calendar with conflict detection. Same software, different posture.

  4. IV.

    It is one surface.

    Students, bookings, finance, inventory, marketing, repairs — one login, one URL, one line in the P&L for software.
