06 · The promise

Don't like something?
We change it.

Every Plannivo customer gets unlimited updates — no tiers, no change-request fees, no "it's on the roadmap". If any part of the platform doesn't fit the way your school runs, tell us and we make it right for you. We crafted Plannivo around our own academy. We'll craft it around yours.

  • Unlimited updates
  • Standard for every customer
  • Built around your school

The cap that keeps it true

A maximum of 10 schools. Ever.

Unlimited updates only stay unlimited if the roster stays small. So Plannivo takes on ten customers, maximum — on purpose. Fewer schools means every update ships fast and every request reaches the people who built the platform, never a ticket queue. When the tenth slot is taken, we close the doors and look after the ten we have.
