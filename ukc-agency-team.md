This manifest defines the specialized AI agent team assembled for the **UKC.world** project. These agents are installed as Antigravity Skills and can be activated at any time.

| Role | Agent Slug | Core Expertise |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

## 🛠️ How to Activate

To call upon a specialist, simply mention them in our conversation:

> "Hey Antigravity, **activate agency-frontend-developer** and help me optimize the Academy page's mobile responsiveness."

> "Let's have the **agency-security-engineer** review our booking form's input validation."
